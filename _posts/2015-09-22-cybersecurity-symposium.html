Join USF for a critical conversation about cybersecurity in today's globally connected world as leaders from government, business, and security discuss the ethical, social, and legal implications of information technology and the nature of increasingly sophisticated cyberattacks.

USF Symposium on Cyber Security

Wednesday, October 14th
Fromm Hall, Xavier Auditorium

  • 5:00pm – Event Registration RSVP Required

  • 5:30pm – Panel I: Ethical, Moral, and Legal Implications of Cyber Attacks. Features John Callas (Silent Circle), Nate Cardozo (EFF), Susan Freiwald (USF), and Jonathan Reiber (Office of the Secretary of Defense).

  • 6:30pm – Panel II: Investing in Security: The Industry and Emerging Technologies. Features Bab Ackerman (Allegis Capital), Joe Levy (Sophos), Barmak Meftah (Alienvault), and Danny Yadron (Wall Street Journal).

  • 7:30pm – Reception

The Symposium on Cyber Security is hosted by the College of Arts and Sciences. See the events calendar for additional details and contact information.
