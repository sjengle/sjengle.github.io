Graduate Advising

Graduate advising week is held one week prior to graduate course registration. During this week there will be a mass advising session and a limited number of one-on-one advising appointments. (Graduate students will receive a signup link via email.)

If you have a question that needs to be addressed before graduate advising week, please stop by my office hours or contact me to schedule an appointment.
