Office Hours

All students are strongly encouraged to take advantage of office hours–it is part of the university experience covered by tuition. Office hours are dedicated times when instructors are available to meet exclusively with students one-on-one or in small groups outside of class time. Students may ask for course-related help, as well as seek academic and professional advice related to the major.

During my office hours, no appointment is necessary. I meet with students first come first served order, alternating between students visiting in person or remotely via Zoom if necessary.
